Designing Composites

Designing composite materials as per the application requirements is fundamentally a challenging and time consuming task. A computational framework based on a deep neural network can be used here to solve the forward (predictive) and reverse (generative) design challenge.

Composite representation


Either select the cells to fill the material with fibre in the matrix of the composite or Input the no of cells to be randomly filled with fibers and generate the composite.






               
               
               
               
               
               
               
               





What the Model does

Composite predictive model:

The forward model predicts the mechanical properties of the composite material with input fiber alignment. The model is trained on the data obtained from the FEM simulations on different compositions and configurations of the composite materials.

What the Model does

Composite Generative Model:

Given the mechanical properties of the composite material, the generative model designs the composition and configuration of matrix and fiber in the composite based on the constraints. This model can also be applied to minimise the fibres necessary to achieve mechanical properties, and the number of fibres can also be included as a constraint with other mechanical properties.
